05

How they behave

The colony runs itself. Each creature follows its own uncertain path, meets others, and comes away quietly changed.

Contact-point dyeing

逐格染色

When two meet, one's cells take on the other's palette from the touch point, sweeping across — outline unchanged.

Matter exchange

互换物质

Bonded partners ferry mosaic tiles back and forth, bowing to opposite sides — a visible circulation of substance.

Free bonds

自由结伴

Compatible creatures drift together, hold for a while, then cool off and leave. No one is tethered.

Mother-tree support

母树机制

A creature on stage long enough quietly becomes a mother tree, reaching a warm line to anyone left alone too long.

Permanent hybridisation

永久杂交

A long encounter leaves a small, permanent trace of the partner's palette — changed by a relationship, not fused.

Blink & gaze

眨眼 · 注视

Each blinks on its own rhythm and glances toward its nearest neighbour — the field feels watched-over, alive.

07

System & determinism

Reproducible
Shape & colour are derived from xmur3 hash → LCG stream. Same sentence, any device, any refresh → the identical spore.
Claimable
A spore's full reading encodes into its URL (?s=), so a link reproduces the exact creature — and exports as a shareable card.
Accumulating
Every whisper persists to a shared store (Upstash Redis), so a fresh visitor opens onto the whole grown ecology and a live population count.
Fast & smooth
One shared canvas for the whole colony; gesture tracking is code-split and loads on demand; spring-eased transitions throughout.
